Pinoy Recipes!

A collection of popular Filipino Dishes and recipes!

Filipinos, are citizens or people identifed with the country of Philippines.

Filipinos, or Pinoy in other term, are popular for their delicious, sumptuous, and flavorful dishes and delicacies.

The majority of Filipino food has a very particular taste between sweet, sour and salty. Like many cuisines, the food of the Philippines evolved for taste and necessity.
